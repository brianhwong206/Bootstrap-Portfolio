About Me

Name: Brian Wong

Education: University of Washington

Degree: Finance and Information Systems

Hello and welcome to my page.

My name is Brian Wong and I was in Seattle, Washington. I went to school at the University of Washington where my areas of focus were Finance and Information Systems from the Michael G. Foster Business School.

During my time at the University of Washington, I worked as a library assistant assigned to the Music Library. I was also a member of UW-BITS (Business Information Technology Society) and the IMA Archery Club. I also took a course abroad in Shanghai, China to study and observe the rapid ascent of the modern Chinese economy.

My hobbies include baseball, photography, commercial aviation, traveling. I am a fan of the Seattle Mariners and have been attending games since they were playing at the Kingdome.

My past work experiences have spanned customer service, retail sales, IT outsourcing, and project management. My current career goals are to improve my technical skills and prepare myself for more data-intensive tasks. I have past experience with data visualization software such as Tableau and Power BI to create, develop, and maintain dashboards.

I've lived abroad in Beijing, China for several years in order to practice my Chinese Mandarin and to have a personal viewpoint of the rapid development of the Chinese economy.
